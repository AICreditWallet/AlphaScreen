# AlphaScreen Promotional Video Storyboard
## Title: The Death of the Blockbuster Budget
## Goal: To demonstrate Hollywood disruption and validate the AlphaScreen vision for investors.

---

### Scene 1: The Ghost of Hollywood Past (0:00 - 0:10)
**Visual:** A dark, dusty, traditional Hollywood soundstage. Empty chairs, expensive-looking camera rigs sitting idle. 
**Audio:** Atmospheric, haunting soundscape. The sound of a projector clicking.
**Text Overlay:** "The old way cost $200 million."

### Scene 2: The Spark of Autonomy (0:10 - 0:25)
**Visual:** A single laptop screen glows in a dark room. A script is being typed: *"EXT. NEON CITY - NIGHT. JAX STANDS IN THE RAIN."*
**Visual (Transition):** The screen explodes into a flurry of code that morphs into a high-fidelity 4K shot of the scene described.
**Audio:** Fast-paced, rhythmic synth music starts building.
**Text Overlay:** "One Script. Zero Crews."

### Scene 3: The Identity Lock (0:25 - 0:45)
**Visual:** Split screen showing 4 different shots of the same character ("Jax") in different locations: a desert, a cockpit, a rain-slicked street, and a royal banquet. The face is 100% consistent.
**Audio:** Jax's voice (ElevenLabs): *"My identity is my own. I am constant in an ever-changing world."*
**Text Overlay:** "Unbreakable Continuity."

### Scene 4: The Scale of Dreams (0:45 - 1:10)
**Visual:** A montage of epic shots—space battles, historical dramas, intimate romance, and surreal landscapes—all rendered in perfect 4K anamorphic quality.
**Audio:** Orchestral peak. Cinematic crescendo.
**Text Overlay:** "2 Hours of Cinema. Autonomous."

### Scene 5: The Call to Visionaries (1:10 - 1:20)
**Visual:** A regular creator (diverse representation) hits a button labeled "MASTER EXPORT." The laptop screen shows "EXPORTING 4K MASTERPIECE."
**Audio:** Fade to a deep, bass-heavy ambient hum.
**Text Overlay:** "AlphaScreen. Pro. Disrupting Cinema."
**Final Text:** "Apply for Production Access at alphascreen.pro"
